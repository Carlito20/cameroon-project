üîÑ How the Business Process Flows

1 Product Selection

We identify essential products that families in Cameroon need - soaps, lotions, baby items, cleaning supplies, basic electronics, and food items.

2 Online Purchase (USA/Canada)

Our brother in USA and sister in Canada purchase products from online retailers and suppliers at competitive prices.

3 Consolidation & Shipping

Products are consolidated and shipped to Cameroon via freight forwarders - either by sea or air depending on urgency and volume.

4 Customs Clearance

We handle all import documentation, customs duties, and taxes to legally bring products into Cameroon.

5 Storage & Inventory

Products are stored in our warehouse in Cameroon with proper inventory tracking and organization.

6 Sales & Distribution

We sell to customers through our physical store, online website, WhatsApp, and bulk orders to businesses.

üí∞ Simple Financial Overview

Where Money Comes From (Revenue)

  • Retail sales to individual customers
  • Bulk orders from small businesses and retailers
  • Online sales through website and social media

Where Money Goes (Costs)

  • Product Purchase: Buying items from USA/Canada online stores
  • Shipping: Freight forwarding from North America to Cameroon
  • Import Duties: Customs duties (0-30% depending on product) and VAT (19.25%)
  • Storage: Warehouse rent and utilities in Cameroon
  • Operations: Marketing, staff, business expenses

How We Make Profit

Selling Price = Product Cost + Shipping + Duties/Taxes + Operating Costs + Profit Margin

We aim to price competitively while maintaining quality and ensuring the business remains sustainable.
